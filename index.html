How to add your own content

  1. Add flashcards by editing data/flashcards.json.
  2. Drop audio files into audio/ and list them in data/audio.json.
  3. Drop PDFs and images into material/ and list them in data/materials.json.
  4. Commit & push — GitHub Pages updates automatically. Share your link!
